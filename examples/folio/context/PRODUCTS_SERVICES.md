# Products & Services — Folio

---

## Product 1 — Folio Starter

### What it is
The entry-level plan. Gives a single agency a branded client portal where they can share deliverables, collect written feedback, and get client approvals. Up to 5 active projects at any time.

### Who it's for
Freelancers and small agencies (1–5 people) who are just starting to feel the pain of email-based client delivery and want a more professional way to present their work. Often their first paid client portal tool.

### What problem it solves
Stops the "I'll just send a Dropbox link" habit that makes agencies look less polished than their work deserves. Gives clients one place to go, and agencies one place to manage the conversation.

### Key benefits
- Clients see everything in one place — no digging through email
- Feedback on files arrives in context, not in a separate email chain
- Approvals are logged with a timestamp — no more "I thought we agreed on version 2"
- Looks professional without requiring a graphic designer to set up

### Key features
- **Branded portal:** Logo, cover image, custom URL (yourname.folio.app)
- **File sharing:** Upload any file type. Image, PDF, video preview built in.
- **Contextual feedback:** Clients annotate directly on images and PDFs
- **Approval tracking:** One-click client approval with timestamp and email confirmation
- **No client login required:** Clients access via a magic link — no account creation

### Pricing
$79/month (or $63/month billed annually). Up to 5 active projects. 1 team seat.

### How it's delivered
SaaS — available immediately on sign-up. No setup call required.

### Timeline / what to expect
Most agencies have their first project portal live and shared with a client within 20 minutes of signing up. Value is usually felt within the first client interaction.

### Proof points
- 68% of Starter customers share their first portal with a client within 24 hours of signing up
- Average first-response time from clients drops from 3.2 days (email) to 6 hours (Folio)

### Common objections & responses

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "My clients won't use a new tool" | They don't have to learn anything — they click a link. No account, no app. If they can open a webpage, they can use Folio. |
| "I already use Notion for this" | Notion works great for internal docs. It wasn't built for client review and approval, and clients feel that. Folio was built specifically for this one job. |
| "It's too expensive for just 5 projects" | At $79/month, if it speeds up one approval and moves one invoice forward by a week, it pays for itself. Most customers feel it within the first month. |

### What it is NOT
- Not a project management tool — there's no task list, no Gantt chart, no internal workflow tracking
- Not a CRM — no client contact database or pipeline management
- Doesn't replace your internal tools (Linear, Asana, etc.)

## Product 2 — Folio Growth

### What it is
The mid-tier plan for established agencies with multiple active clients. Unlimited active projects, up to 3 team seats, and more control over the client experience.

### Who it's for
Agencies with 5–20 staff who have outgrown Starter or are signing up already at this scale. The person managing multiple project managers and multiple active client relationships at once.

### What problem it solves
Removes the artificial constraint of 5 active projects (Starter) and adds team collaboration — multiple people can manage portals and respond to client feedback.

### Key benefits
- No project limit — run as many active portals as you have clients
- 3 team members can manage portals (project managers, account managers, designers)
- Internal comments visible only to the team — separate from client-visible feedback
- Client activity notifications — know when a client has opened and reviewed work without asking

### Key features
- Everything in Starter, plus:
- **Unlimited projects**
- **3 team seats**
- **Internal comments:** Team-only notes on feedback and approvals — keep context without cluttering the client view
- **Activity dashboard:** See at a glance which portals need attention (awaiting client review, client viewed but hasn't responded, approval overdue)
- **Email digest:** Daily or weekly summary of client activity across all portals

### Pricing
$199/month (or $159/month billed annually). Unlimited projects. 3 team seats.

### How it's delivered
SaaS. Upgrades from Starter instantly — all existing projects carry over.

### Proof points
- Agencies on Growth tier report their project managers spend an average of 5 fewer hours per week on client coordination
- 84% of Growth customers renew annually

### Common objections & responses

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I don't need 3 seats — it's just me managing client comms" | If that's sustainable, Starter may be the right call. Growth makes sense when you're spreading delivery across more than one person. |
| "That's a big jump from Starter" | It is. If 5 projects is genuinely enough, stay on Starter. Growth is for agencies where the constraint is the team cap, not just the project cap. |

### What it is NOT
Not white-label — portals are branded with the agency's logo but Folio branding is present. (That's Team tier.)

## Product 3 — Folio Team

### What it is
The full-featured plan with white-label portals, up to 10 seats, priority support, and onboarding assistance. Designed for agencies who want Folio to be invisible — an extension of their own brand.

### Who it's for
Larger agencies (15–60 staff) and agencies building Folio into a productised service offering. Also agencies with high-value retainer clients who expect a polished, branded experience.

### Key benefits
- Portals are fully white-labelled — Folio branding removed entirely, custom domain (clients.youragency.com)
- 10 team seats — enough for the whole delivery team
- Priority support with a named CSM for onboarding and ongoing check-ins
- Early access to new features

### Key features
- Everything in Growth, plus:
- **White label:** Remove all Folio branding. Custom domain. Clients see only your brand.
- **10 team seats**
- **Priority support + CSM**
- **SSO (coming Q3 2026)**
- **Advanced permissions:** Control exactly what each team member and client can see and do

### Pricing
$399/month (or $319/month billed annually). Up to 10 seats. Custom pricing available for larger teams.

### How it's delivered
SaaS. Onboarding call included and recommended for white-label setup.

### Timeline / what to expect
White-label setup typically takes 30 minutes with guidance. CSM does a 45-minute onboarding call to ensure everything is configured correctly.

### Common objections & responses

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We need SSO" | It's on the roadmap for Q3 2026. If SSO is a hard requirement today, we're honest that we're not the right fit yet. |
| "Can we get more than 10 seats?" | Yes — custom pricing available. Contact us directly. |

### What it is NOT
- Not enterprise — no detailed audit logs, no SAML SSO (yet), no custom procurement process

## Pricing Philosophy

We price based on the value we deliver, not on how many features we can bundle. Starter exists to let people experience the product; it's not a watered-down trap. Growth and Team are priced to be affordable for the agencies they're built for. We don't do discounts to close deals — if someone needs a discount, it's usually a sign they're not the right customer yet.

---

## Things We Don't Do

- We don't offer a free plan — the product requires real setup and real client interactions to show value. A free plan creates the wrong expectations.
- We don't offer 24/7 live support — support is by email and Intercom chat during business hours (9am–6pm EST). Typical response time: under 2 hours.
- We don't do custom feature development for individual customers.
- We don't support native integrations with every tool — current integrations: Zapier, Slack, HubSpot. More coming.
