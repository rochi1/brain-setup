### Competitive claims — what we can and can't say

*Help AI avoid making claims you can't support.*

| Claim | Can we say it? | Evidence / caveat |
|-------|---------------|-------------------|
| *"We're the fastest"* | *No — unverified* | *Say "one of the fastest" or give specific benchmarks* |
| *"Market leader"* | *Only in [specific segment]* | *Qualify the claim* |
| *"Best-in-class support"* | *Yes* | *[Review score / NPS / specific proof]* |

## Win / Loss Patterns

*If you track why you win or lose deals, capture the patterns here. Extremely useful for AI writing sales content.*

**We typically win when:**
- *e.g. The customer has tried [alternative] and hit its limits*
- *e.g. The buyer cares more about [outcome] than [price/feature]*
- *e.g. There's an internal champion who has used us before*

**We typically lose when:**
- *e.g. Price is the only decision criterion*
- *e.g. The customer needs [feature we don't have yet]*
- *e.g. The deal is already decided before we're brought in*
