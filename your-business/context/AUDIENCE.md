# Audience

> **How to use this file:** Define who your customers are. AI tools use this to tailor language, focus on the right pain points, and avoid pitching to the wrong people.
>
> **First time?** Use the Audience prompt in [onboarding/QUICK_START.md](../../onboarding/QUICK_START.md) — an AI will interview you persona by persona and generate this file automatically. Or fill in the sections below directly.

---

## How to Use Personas

Each persona represents a real segment of your customer base — not a fictional ideal. Base them on what you actually observe: sales calls, support tickets, reviews, interviews. The more grounded they are, the more useful they are to AI.

When prompting AI, you can say: *"Write this for [Persona Name]"* and it will adjust accordingly.

## Customer Lifecycle Stages

*How does someone move from stranger to loyal customer? Define the stages as they apply to your business.*

| Stage | What they know about us | What they need | How we communicate |
|-------|------------------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Unaware | Nothing | To discover us | *e.g. Educational content, no pitch* |
| Aware | We exist | To understand what we do | *e.g. Clear explainers, proof* |
| Considering | What we offer | To evaluate us vs. alternatives | *e.g. Case studies, demos, comparisons* |
| Customer | They've bought | To get value quickly | *e.g. Onboarding, support* |
| Loyal | Deep experience | To expand or advocate | *e.g. Upsell, referral, community* |
