# Custom Roles

> **What this file is:** Your business-specific role definitions. When an AI loads this alongside your Master Prompt, it can step into any of these roles on demand.
>
> **How to generate a role:** Use the custom role prompt in [prompts/ROLES.md](../../prompts/ROLES.md) — an AI will interview you and produce a ready-to-paste role block. Save it here.
>
> **How to use:** Copy a role block and paste it after your Master Prompt, before your task. e.g. *"[Master Prompt] + [Role: Your Role] + [Task: do the thing]"*
