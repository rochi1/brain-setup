# Your Business — AI Context Files

> **This folder is yours.** Fill in these files with your real business information. This is what you load into your AI tools, upload to a project knowledge base, or share with your team.

---

## What's in Here

| File | What it's for |
|------|--------------|
| `context/BUSINESS_PROFILE.md` | Who you are, what you do, your mission and values |
| `context/BRAND_VOICE.md` | Tone, language rules, do/don't guidelines |
| `context/AUDIENCE.md` | Customer personas and how they think |
| `context/PRODUCTS_SERVICES.md` | What you sell and how to describe it accurately |
| `context/COMPETITORS.md` | Market context and competitive positioning |
| `prompts/MASTER_PROMPT.md` | The single combined prompt your team uses every day |
| `governance/AI_POLICY.md` | Your team's rules for using AI |
| `governance/DATA_RULES.md` | What data is safe to share with AI tools |

---

## How to Fill These In

Use the prompts in [onboarding/QUICK_START.md](../onboarding/QUICK_START.md). Work through the 4 steps in order — each generates a completed file you save back here.

Blank template versions of every file live in `templates/` if you ever need to start fresh.

## Privacy Note

These files will contain real information about your business — positioning, customers, pricing, and policy. If this repo is public or shared, consider:
- Keeping this folder in a **separate private repo** and referencing the kit from there
- Adding `your-business/` to `.gitignore` if you don't want it committed

The rest of the repo (templates, guides, examples) contains no business-specific information and is safe to share.
